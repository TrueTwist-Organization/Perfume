The Secret Garden

Luna Noire was designed for the quiet moments before sleep. It is a scent of pure serenity, capturing the silvery light of the moon reflecting off white petals in a hidden night garden.

The inspiration came from a midnight stroll through a lavender field in Provence, where the only light was that of the full moon. We wanted to bottle that feeling of ethereal peace and dreamy mystery.
