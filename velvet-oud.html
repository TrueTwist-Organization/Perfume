A Legacy Inscribed in Gold

Velvet Oud was born from a desire to capture the essence of a midnight desert palace. It is a scent of power and mystery, designed for those who command presence without saying a word.

The story begins with the finest agarwood sourced from the depths of the Assam forests, aged for two decades to achieve its characteristic velvety depth. We blended this "Liquid Gold" with the delicate sweetness of Taif Rose, creating a symphony of contrasts that is both ancient and modern.

Agarwood (Oud)
Origin: Assam, India

Sourced from diseased Aquilaria trees, this resinous heartwood produces a complex, woody, and animalic scent that is more valuable than gold.

Iranian Saffron
Origin: Khorasan

Known as 'Red Gold', our saffron adds a leathery, bittersweet, and earthy tone that gives Velvet Oud its initial brilliance.

Taif Rose
Origin: Saudi Arabia

Cultivated in the majestic mountains of Taif, these roses offer a uniquely potent, spicy, and honey-like floral aroma.
