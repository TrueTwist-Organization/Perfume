The Art of Scent

Fragrance Notes

Every perfume unfolds in three acts — each note harmonizing to create an unforgettable olfactory experience.

🌸

Top Notes

The first impression. Bright, volatile bursts of bergamot, pink pepper, saffron, and citrus that captivate within seconds of application.

🌹

Heart Notes

The soul of the fragrance. Rich florals and spices — Bulgarian rose, jasmine sambac, oud, and iris — emerge as top notes fade.

🪵

Base Notes

The lasting memory. Warm, deep anchors of sandalwood, amber, musk, and vanilla that linger on skin for 12+ hours.

✨

Sillage

The invisible trail you leave behind. Our perfumes are crafted for a beautiful, moderate sillage that captivates without overwhelming.

Our Heritage

Crafted with Passion, Worn with Elegance

Born from a deep reverence for the art of perfumery, Noir Essence was founded in 2018 by a collective of master perfumers trained in Grasse, France — the world's perfume capital.

Every bottle represents hundreds of hours of meticulous craftsmanship. We source the rarest ingredients — Mysore sandalwood, Omani frankincense, Bulgarian rose otto — and blend them using time-honored French techniques.
